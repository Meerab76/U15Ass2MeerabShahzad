Welcome to Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum

At Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum, we are committed to creating an enriching environment for Slovak families in South Manchester. We aim to preserve and promote Slovak language and culture through education, community activities, and cultural events.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality education to Slovak-speaking children and to create a safe and inclusive space where Slovak families can connect, learn, and celebrate their heritage. We focus on teaching the Slovak language, exploring cultural traditions, and encouraging participation in community events.

Our History

Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum was founded in 2010 with the goal of supporting Slovak families living in South Manchester. Over the years, we have grown to become a key cultural institution, offering Slovak language classes, workshops, and cultural events for both children and adults. Our centre continues to thrive with the help of passionate educators and volunteers who are dedicated to making a difference in the lives of our community members.

Our Values

At Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum, we value:

  • Education: We believe in the power of education to connect people, build understanding, and empower individuals to thrive.
  • Community: We foster a welcoming environment that celebrates diversity and encourages collaboration among our members.
  • Heritage: We are committed to preserving and promoting Slovak traditions, culture, and language.
  • Respect: We respect the individuality of each person and strive to create an inclusive environment for everyone.
Our Impact

Through our language classes, cultural events, and community activities, we have made a lasting impact on the Slovak-speaking community in South Manchester. Our programs help children develop a strong connection to their Slovak heritage, while adults can engage in lifelong learning and connect with others who share their cultural background. We are proud to be a vital part of the Slovak diaspora and continue to grow with the support of our dedicated volunteers, teachers, and community members.

Get Involved with Us

We are always looking for passionate individuals to join our mission. Whether you are interested in volunteering, attending events, or learning more about Slovak culture, there is a place for you at Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum.
