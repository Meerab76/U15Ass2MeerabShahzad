Welcome to Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum

At Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum, we are committed to creating an enriching environment for Slovak families in South Manchester. Our mission is to promote Slovak language and culture, offering educational and cultural activities for all ages. Whether you're looking to maintain your Slovak heritage or learn something new, we are here to support you!

What We Do

In addition to our educational programs, we host a variety of events including cultural festivals, social gatherings, and seasonal workshops. Our center provides a space for learning, celebrating, and connecting with others who share the same cultural background.

Our Values

We believe that language is not just a way of communicating but an essential part of one's identity. Through our work, we help preserve Slovak traditions and values, fostering a strong sense of belonging within the community.
