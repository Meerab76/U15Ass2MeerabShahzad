Welcome to Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum

At Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum, we are committed to creating an enriching environment for Slovak families in South Manchester. Our focus is on empowering the Slovak-speaking community through education, cultural engagement, and fostering connections between people who share similar backgrounds and values.

Our Vision

Our vision is to establish a vibrant and inclusive community where Slovak traditions, language, and culture are not only celebrated but passed down to future generations. We are here to make sure that children of Slovak-speaking parents maintain a strong cultural identity and connection to Slovakia, regardless of where they are in the world.

Our Approach

At Slovenské Vzdelávacie Centrum, we believe in learning through experience. While our educational programs focus on language development, we go beyond that by involving our students in a variety of activities, including cultural workshops, festivals, and field trips. By immersing our students in Slovak culture, they learn about the importance of heritage, family, and community.

Why Choose Us?

We are not just a school. We are a place where people from all walks of life come together to form lasting friendships, celebrate Slovak traditions, and share their experiences. Our highly experienced educators are passionate about making learning fun and interactive. Whether you're a child or an adult, we welcome everyone who wants to learn the Slovak language and culture.

Get Involved

We are always looking for new members to join our community, whether you're looking to learn the Slovak language, participate in events, or simply connect with others who share your heritage. We also invite volunteers who want to contribute to our mission and help us build a strong and supportive environment for Slovak families. Your involvement makes all the difference!
